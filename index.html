  • Startup Weekend Vancouver

    Friday, Nov 14th

    6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

    Startup Weekend is a global grassroots movement of active and empowered entrepreneurs who are learning the basics of founding startups and launching successful ventures. It is the largest community of passionate entrepreneurs with over 1800 past events in 120 countries around the world in 2014.

    We begin with open mic pitches on Friday where attendees bring their best ideas and inspire others to join their team. Over Saturday and Sunday teams focus on customer development, validating their ideas, practicing LEAN Startup Methodologies and building a minimal viable product. On Sunday evening teams demo their prototypes and receive valuable feedback from a panel of experts.

  • Fireside Chat with Dan Martell

    Tuesday, Nov 18th

    6:30 PM - 9:30 PM

He previously co-founded Flowtown, a San Francisco based social marketing product which raised funding, grew to over 50,000 small business customers and was eventually acquired by Demandforce in 2011.   • Collaboration, the New Economy

    Thursday, Nov 20th

    5:30 PM - 10:00 PM

    Presented by Credit Unions of BC.

    "We used to live in a world where there are people, private citizens, a world where there are businesses, and now we're living in a world where people can become businesses in 60 seconds"
      - Brian Chesky, CEO, AirBNB

    Today, if you have that killer idea, all you need is a credit card and a cellphone to start your business.

    With the collaborative economy, you no longer need the same depth in resources once required to get your business off the ground. The collaborative economy is the connector and the solution to many of the challenges startups face.

    This session will help you:

    Learn about the latest developments and thinking in the collaborative economy
    Understand how startups can setup a cooperative business model that impacts society
    Leverage cost-effective resources and management practices to create branding, MVP, distribute and market what they have built

    Speakers

    Nik Badminton, Regional Director, North America for freelancer.com
    Alexandra Samuel, VP of Social Media, Vision Critical
    Martin Reed - AVP, Marketing & Research Central 1 Credit Union
    Moderator: Meena Sandhu, Marketing Strategist and Speaker
